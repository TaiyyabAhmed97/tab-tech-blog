starting from سورة الفاطر

* ayat 1 ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ فَاطِرِ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ
	* why ال cant be for عهد: because that would be a specific حمد whereas ال here can come for استغراق or جنس
	* Not all angels are messengers as is known
* ayat 2 مَّا يَفْتَحِ ٱللَّهُ لِلنَّاسِ
	* الرحمة: here specifically can refer to rain or sustenance
	* Allah is العزيز in His ovepowering in his command and الحكيم in His actions.
* ayat 3 ٢ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلنَّاسُ ٱذْكُرُوا نِعْمَتَ ٱللَّهِ عَلَيْكُمْ :
	* specifically people of makkah
	* what are نعمت الله عليكم them able to live in makkah and stopping of fighting there?
	* why is can غير الله be read with kasra here (riwayat of حمزة)
* ayat 4 وَإِن يُكَذِّبُوكَ فَقَدْ كُذِّبَتْ:
	* consolation
		* if they belied you, then same thing has happened to previous prophets, nothing new here, they went through similar trials, so so are you
	*  so ترجع الامور means Allah will recompense the believers with good and disbelievers with punishment
* ayat 8 أَفَمَن زُيِّنَ لَهُۥ سُوٓءُ عَمَلِهِۦ:
	* sabab an nuzul: abu jahl
* ayat 9 وَٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَرْسَلَ ٱلرِّيَـٰحَ:
	* there are 3 التفات going on here after ارسل الرياح
		1. f فتثير (moves)
		2. f فسقناه (waters)
		3. f فاحييناه (bring to life)
	* s كذالك so same way you will be resurected
* ayat 10 مَن كَانَ يُرِيدُ ٱلْعِزَّةَ فَلِلَّهِ ٱلْعِزَّةُ جَمِيعًا ۚ:
	* the ones who did makr asayyiaat 
		* sabab- the people of دار الندوة where they plotted to kill/expel
			* surat anfaal ayat 30
	* y يبور -> يهلك
* ayat 11 وَاللَّهُ خَلَقَكُم مِّن تُرَابٍ ثُمَّ:
	* another proof plus ayat 9 of resurrection and Allah being sole creator and sustainer
		* ties into theme and surat title (فاطر)
* ayat 12 وَمَا يَسْتَوِى ٱلْبَحْرَانِ:
	* Allah examples seas 
		* fresh and salt
			* only Allah know the exact differences and no one else could ever know
		* even though same things happen in there (fish eat and we eat fish from them, ride ships on both of them such that the tide pulls back and forth مواخر)
		* why we do all those actions on seas
			* to obtain Allahs treasures and be thankful
	* membrane of date seed
* ayat 16 إِن يَشَأْ يُذْهِبْكُمْ:
	* a من عرف نفسه فقد عرف ربه (because once you understand your own lowness and faqeerness, then you understand Allah's indepenence and height of his oneness)
* ayat 17 وَمَا ذَٰلِكَ عَلَى اللَّهِ بِعَزِيزٍ :
	* That is not something difficult for Allah.
* ayat 18 وَلَا تَزِرُ وَازِرةٌ:
	* e نفس is موصوف محذوف for وازرة
* ayat 25:
	* a Definitely by disbelieving in one prophet, you have disbelieved all of them
	* a تسلية -> so have patience like they(all previous prophets) had patience.
* ayat 26: 
	* a التفلت -> He is the one who brings down rain, We are the ones who bring out different fruits with different colors
		* reason for التفات <- الاخرج is a greater and more miraculous than bringing down rain and is a proof of the power of Allah.
	* a جدد -> طريقة في الجبل
