## Who is Hajj واجب upon (6 conditions for male and female)

1. Free
2. Baaligh
3. Sane
4. Healthy
5. Capable of enough wealth and transportation  
	* 2 subconditions:  
		* able to leave from his home
		* able to leave enough for his family till his return
6. His Travel journey is safe  

Proof of Hajj's فرضية from Al-Quran:
و لله عل الناس surat Al-Imran 97
